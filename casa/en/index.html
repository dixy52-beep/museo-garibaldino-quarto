Garibaldi's House in Genoa

Discover the historic Villa Spinola, now the Garibaldino Museum

The Real House of Giuseppe Garibaldi in Genoa

Are you looking for "Garibaldi's house" in Genoa? You've found it. This is the historic Villa Spinola in the Quarto district, one of the most famous and important villas in Genoa connected to the Italian Risorgimento.

This residence was not just a temporary stop; it became the true home of Giuseppe Garibaldi and his headquarters during the planning of the Expedition of the Thousand. Today, this iconic location houses the official Garibaldino Museum.

A Dive into History

Visit the rooms of Villa Spinola where Garibaldi planned the Expedition. Walk where history was written and feel the excitement of those crucial moments.

Authentic Relics

The museum houses original uniforms, weapons, letters, and documents belonging to the Thousand. These are objects that tell stories of courage and sacrifice.
